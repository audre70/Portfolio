Hi, Nice to meet you!

I’m Audrey. I’m a Presidential Honors Scholar at New York University in my senior year, majoring in International Relations and History with a minor in French.

I previously interned at iHeartRadio as a UX Designer. I also worked at the nonprofit Housing Rights Initiative and New York City Council.

In my free time, I enjoy baking, going to parks, caring for dogs, making art, and playing video games. My all-time favorites are Zelda Tears of the Kingdom and Minecraft.

Why tech?

I'm interested in the intersection of technology and business and am curious about the constantly innovative nature of the field, especially in the entertainment, streaming, and gaming spaces. I've enjoyed working on both the product and design side of teams, building engaging products through research, creativity, and data-informed approaches.

In the future, I am eager to blend my passions for tech and the law as in-house counsel at a tech company after my postgraduate studies.
